 | Also, is there any reason why you define MU and NU as syntax, and
 | not as procedures?

(define mu*
  (lambda args
    (lambda (f) (apply f args))))

((mu* 1 2 3) list)			=> (1 2 3)
((apply mu* 1 '(2 3)) list)		=> (1 2 3)

Although MU* can be substituted for MU and NU, MU* is much slower than MU.
