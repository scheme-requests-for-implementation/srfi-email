To use SRFI-57 with MzScheme native records, simply prepend the following lines to the reference implementation: 

(module srfi-9 mzscheme
  (require (lib "9.ss" "srfi"))
  (provide (rename define-record-type srfi-9:define-record-type)))

(require srfi-9)
