SRFI-105 expected to be in next guile release

This page is part of the web mail archives of SRFI 105 from before July 7th, 2015. The new archives for SRFI 105 contain all messages, not just those from before July 7th, 2015.

  • Delivered-to: srfi-105@xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

FYI, Ludovic Courtès is proposing the that guile version 2.0.7 be released relatively soon; he's suggesting that it be scheduled for "week 48".  The good news for us is that this is expected to include an implementation of SRFI-105... which means that we're already getting traction.

My sincere thanks to Mark H Weaver and all others involved in getting this into guile!

I hope that many other implementations will soon follow....!

--- David A. Wheeler
