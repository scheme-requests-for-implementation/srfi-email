All: Unless we hear of any changes, let's complete SRFI-105 by the end of November 1, 2012, U.S. East Coast Time.

I'd like to do it sooner, but I realize that we have other challenges, e.g.: (1) the srfi.schemers.org website was down, and (2) Hurricane Sandy may interfere with communications of people on the U.S. east coast.

I cannot imagine accepting a proposal at this point that would change the *semantics* of SRFI-105; what's done is done.  But I think we should entertain fixes in the design rationale (since we want the rationale to be right) and typos for just a few more days, and then declare that we're done.

--- David A. Wheeler
