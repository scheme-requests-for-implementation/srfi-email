If you are implementing SRFI-105 (or are about to start), please post to the mailing list, telling us that you're doing it and for what Scheme implementation.  If it's open source software, please also tell us where your repository is.

That should help reduce duplication of effort, and hopefully will help collaboration.  It would also encourage others to also implement SRFI-105; it's important to me that MANY implementations include SRFI-105 (for portability).

We already know that guile has SRFI-105 in its development branch (to be included in the next release), and that there's ongoing work for Sagittarius, so no need to post about those.
