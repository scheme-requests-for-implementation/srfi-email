I'd like to know why multiple-values syntax can not be used in `named let'.

(srfi-let lp ((a 1) (b 2) (c 3)) (list a b c))	---> (1 2 3)
(srfi-let lp ((a b c (values 1 2 3))) (list a b c))  ---> bad syntax error
