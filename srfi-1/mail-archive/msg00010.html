May I suggest the following addition to the SRFI-1 list library, Common Lisp's
LDIFF:

I think a reasonable definition is:


(define (ldiff l subl)
  (cond
   ((null? l) '())
   ((eq? (cdr l) subl) (list (car l)))
   (else (cons (car l)
	       (ldiff (cdr l) subl)))))
