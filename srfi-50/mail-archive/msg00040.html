SCHEME_FALSE, SCHEME_TRUE, SCHEME_NULL, and SCHEME_UNSPECIFIC should
be functions, not constants:


Not

	x = SCHEME_FALSE;

but:

	SCHEME_MAKE_FALSE (&x, instance);

because, you never know, those constants might be heap allocated.

-t
