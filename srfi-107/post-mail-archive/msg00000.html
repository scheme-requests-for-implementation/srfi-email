SRFI-107 was finalized on 12/22.

Thanks to all who participated with comments and suggestions,
most notably John Cowan, who provided much good feedback.
Also, thanks to our responsive and helpful editor Mike Sperber.

SRFI 107 is implemented in Kawa.  If someone else is implementing it,
sending a note to this list seems appropriate.

I submitted a trio of SRFIs on 2012/11/03.  It is interesting that the
one that got the lowest number and I though was most ready (because
there was an existing implementation) ended up taking the longest.
