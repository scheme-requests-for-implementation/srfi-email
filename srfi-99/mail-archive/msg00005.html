Ray Blaak wrote:

Ok:
<definition> -> <record type definition> Nevertheless, my suggestion still stands. For all the helper record methods and forms, something that more directly reads as being record related would be better than "rtd".
Oh -- I forgot to mention: rtd is shorthand for record type descriptor, not record type definition. So your email should be taken as further evidence that the notational assumptions are not well described.
Also, I now realize rtd is used in SRFI 99 names, not to coincide with R6RS, but to avoid name clashes. Perhaps this should be stated early in the specification?
