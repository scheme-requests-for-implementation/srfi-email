I would like to point out that Felix Winkelmann has kindly made available integrated support for SRFI-72 macros as a language extension (egg) for the Chicken Scheme compiler. 

It is available as the 'simple-macros' egg at
http://www.call-with-current-continuation.org/eggs/index.html#lang-exts
Caveat: This extension has been tested on small examples but not yet on nontrivial real-world applications.
