I believe the following are all valid uses of string->date, but each results in an error that char-alphabetic? expects argument of type <character>; given #<eof>. 

   (string->date "2000 1 March" "~Y ~d ~B")
   (string->date "2000 1 March Wednesday" "~Y ~d ~B ~A")
   (string->date "2000 1 Mar" "~Y ~d ~b")
   (string->date "2000 1 March Wed" "~Y ~d ~B ~a")
