Takashi Kato scripsit:

> BTW, why is there no queue appending procedure like
> list-queue-add-back-all!?

I think I'll add list-queue-append and list-queue-append!.  The first
is trivial in the presence of list-concatenate, and the second is
O(N) where N is the number of queues being appended and provides
the function of list-queue-add-back-all!.
